About WIE AG

The IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) Student Branch at Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Hyderabad is a dynamic force advancing gender diversity and empowerment in engineering and technology. By offering leadership development, technical workshops, mentorship, and community engagement, WIE creates an environment where women thrive academically and professionally. It breaks traditional barriers and fosters inclusion and innovation, connecting aspiring women engineers with resources, role models, and opportunities that fuel confidence and success.

Vision

A globally recognized leader in remote sensing for the benefit of humanity.

WIE envisions a future where women are equally represented, recognized, and empowered in all engineering and technology fields. It strives to nurture creativity, resilience, and leadership in women engineers to challenge norms and drive change. Believing diversity fuels true innovation, WIE fosters an environment that celebrates unique talents and inspires equity, excellence, and a forward-thinking engineering landscape.

Mission

The mission of IEEE WIE Student Branch at SIT Hyderabad is to empower female students by providing opportunities to grow technical skills, leadership, and professional networks. Through workshops, hackathons, mentorship, and projects, WIE equips members to excel in STEM careers. Promoting gender equity and inclusion, it breaks social barriers, encourages academic excellence, and partners with industry to prepare socially responsible leaders ready to impact society and engineering.
